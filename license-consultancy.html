FSSAI

The Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI) regulates food safety under the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare. It was established under the Food Safety and Standards Act, 2006.

Import Export License

IEC (Import Export Code) is a 10-digit license issued by the DGFT that enables Indian businesses to trade internationally.

Shop Act (Gumasta)

Required for doing business in Maharashtra, Gumasta is issued under the Maharashtra Shops and Establishment Act by the Municipal Corporation.

Factory License

Mandatory for manufacturing operations. Approval is required from the labor and employment department before registering a factory.

NABL

NABL provides accreditation to testing/calibration labs based on international standards like ISO/IEC 17025 and ISO 15189.

NABH

NABH is a quality council body for accrediting hospitals and healthcare providers to ensure high standards of care and safety.

APEDA

APEDA promotes the export of agricultural and processed food products and was established under the act of Parliament in 1985.

PASARA

PSARA License is needed to start a private security agency in India. It certifies the agency to legally provide security services.

ISI

The ISI mark certifies that a product conforms to Indian standards set by the Bureau of Indian Standards. It’s mandatory for 90+ products.

BIS

BIS certification ensures third-party guarantee of product quality and safety. It's voluntary but mandatory for certain categories by law.
