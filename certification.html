Certification Services

ISO 9001:2015

Specifies requirements for a quality management system to ensure consistent quality in products and services.

ISO 13485:2016

Specifies requirements for a quality system for medical devices and related services.

ISO 14001:2015

Environmental management system standard aimed at enhancing environmental performance.

ISO 45001:2018

Standard for occupational health and safety management systems to reduce workplace risks.

ISO 22000:2018

Food safety management system standard to ensure safe food throughout the supply chain.

ISO 27001:2013

Information security management system standard to safeguard data and reduce risk.

IATF 16949:2016

Automotive industry quality management standard developed by IATF.

ISO 29990:2010

Standard for non-formal education and training services for transparency and global comparison.

HACCP

Defines requirements for effective control of food safety based on seven principles.

GMP

Certification to ensure good manufacturing practices for food and pharmaceutical products.

Halal

Ensures that products meet Islamic dietary laws and are permissible for consumption.

Kosher

Certification for food products meeting Jewish dietary laws, ensuring kosher compliance.

CE

Indicates conformity with EU health, safety, and environmental protection standards.

ROHS

Ensures restriction of hazardous substances in electronic and electrical equipment.

SA 8000

Social certification standard ensuring ethical working conditions globally.

SEDEX

Platform and audit framework for supply chain ethical performance.

FDA

Required certification for products entering the U.S., regulated by the Food and Drug Administration.

CMMI

Model that helps organizations improve performance in product and service development.
